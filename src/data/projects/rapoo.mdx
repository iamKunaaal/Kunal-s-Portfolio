# Rapoo.in: Official Brand & Shopping Experience Platform

## Overview

Rapoo.in is the official Indian website for Rapoo, a global leader in wireless keyboards, mice, headsets, and smart accessories. The platform serves as both a brand hub and an e-commerce solution, enabling customers to explore products, engage with campaigns, and shop seamlessly online.

I worked as the primary developer and maintainer of the platform, including during Rapoo's major collaboration with the Mumbai Indians, where the website required fast updates, branded campaign content, and stable performance under increased visibility.

## What Users Can Do

### Product Discovery & Shopping
- Browse Rapoo's complete range of wireless peripherals
- View detailed product specifications and features
- Purchase products through a smooth WooCommerce-powered store

### Campaign & Collaboration Engagement
- Experience promotional landing pages and seasonal campaigns
- Access Mumbai Indians collaboration branding and product highlights
- Stay updated with new launches and featured collections

### Customer-Friendly Experience
- Fully responsive design across mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Fast navigation and clean UI for product exploration
- Secure and optimized shopping flow

### Admin & Store Management
- Easy product uploads, pricing updates, and stock management
- Custom backend improvements for smoother operations
- Support for marketing banners, homepage sections, and promotions

## Custom Development Contributions

A major part of this project involved custom WordPress and WooCommerce development, including:

- Custom UI components and theme-level enhancements
- WooCommerce checkout and cart experience improvements
- Custom product display layouts and category structures
- Performance optimization for faster page loads
